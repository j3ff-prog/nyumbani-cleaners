✶ About Us

Nairobi's Premium Home Care Service

At Nyumbani Cleaning Services, we believe a clean space creates peace of mind. We are a trusted cleaning company dedicated to providing professional, reliable, and affordable cleaning solutions for homes, offices, apartments, and Airbnb spaces.

Our team is trained to deliver high-quality services including deep cleaning, regular house cleaning, laundry services, move-in/move-out cleaning, and organization services. We pay attention to every detail to ensure your space is spotless, fresh, and comfortable.

Beyond cleaning, we are passionate about empowering communities. Through employment and training opportunities, Nyumbani Cleaning Services has empowered more than 1,000 women across Nairobi, Kenya, helping them earn a living and build a better future for themselves and their families.

At Nyumbani Cleaning, customer satisfaction is our priority, and we take pride in creating clean and healthy environments for our clients across Kenya.

🎯 Our Mission

To redefine home care in Kenya by combining professionalism, reliability, and genuine care — while empowering women with skills and economic opportunity.
